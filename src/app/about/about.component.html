About:

Its a web app for those who want to find, post, and get details on diy shows or big venue concerts. It’s geared towards those who want to be more up to date with intimate house shows that are in their area, and promote the local music they love.

You can post your diy show You can browse local diy shows (and local public shows) And you can get the deets for these gigs! And if you’re hosting a show, you can determine who gets access to your show’s info, so your address isn’t out there for the world to see! You can also, ask for help.
